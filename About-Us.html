About Us

Being established in the year of 2012, Laxmi Enterprises Services Pvt. Ltd. shares an immense pleasure in introducing the coating services to wide variety of products for today's industries like aerospace, commercial, home items, Nuclear, Automotive, Aerospace, General Engineering, Electronic, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Food & Beverage and many more…..

Laxmi Enterprises services is a brainchild of accomplished entrepreneur Mr. Santosh Gajre whose technical, commercial acumen and guidance has given the company the impetus and growth to be financially and technologically progressive.

Laxmi Enterprises services is a highly technology focused company engaged in handling wide variety of blasting and coating projects. We have a variety of speciality coatings like sand blasting that include sand, slot, copper slag, grid and water jetting. We also provide services for structural steel protection through cementitious fireproofing and intumescent coating. Gunniting, Industrial brush painting and all types of airless spray painting helps to enhance your product and make them look better, perform better, and sell better.
